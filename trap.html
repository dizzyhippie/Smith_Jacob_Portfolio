Project

Trap Complex Gaming was a personal project started with a friend during the pandemic. We hosted online gaming tournaments with cash prizes and contents for the participants. The branding, logo, website, and social media posts were designed by me.

Process

We hosted a few tournaments a week, with paid entry and cash prizes. We used social media to advertise these events and posted announcements in our community Discord. Our website was always getting updated with new events and the previous winners.

Outcome

We hosted many tournaments with a large player turnout. Our social media advertising was very effective, and gained a big following behind Trap Complex Gaming. Some of our artwork and posts can be found below!
